HCCDB is a database system for human colorectal cancer information. It mainly includes genes, pathways, drugs and related literature about colorectal cancer. Colorectal cancer remains the third most common cancer in the world and the leading cause of cancer morbidity and mortality. Therefore, it is urgent to further study the pathogenesis of colorectal cancer and establish effective prevention, diagnosis and treatment methods.
