Human colon cancer Database

HccDB is a database system for human colon cancer information. It mainly includes genes, pathways, drugs and related literature about colon cancer. From a genetic standpoint, colon cancer is one of the most well understood cancers. However, colon cancer remains the third most common cancer in the world and the leading cause of cancer morbidity and mortality. Therefore, it is urgent to further study the pathogenesis of colon cancer and establish effective prevention, diagnosis and treatment methods.
